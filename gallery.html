Vision by R.Q.

 

Spent so much time

Basking in plant isoprenes

These eyes of mine

Happen to see things differently

Is vision what I see through

My eyes or is it

My capacity for infinite

Creativity

Crafty fox, stinging

Hard, like hit the hornet nest

Patiently, await what's next

What comes next

In this aimless quest

Contemplate flimsy words

Contentment and Happiness

Though the melancholy

Accompanies

Remnant of the old aristocracy

Who am I?

Samurai, Superfluous Guy

Anachronism

To modern times

In my imagination

I frolic through bountiful

Lands

These days I'm doubtful

Like sell your soul

Make an hourly, collect the payroll

Fill me up with the food of subjugation

Indignation, Indigestion, from this

Food you fed us

Preservatives and artificial flavors

Spit them out, Like a

Horse, I spit the bit

Spit the bit, guess I'll spit a bit

Its said that Michelangelo

Saw an angel in the marble

And set him free

See Kami in shackles aim to

Set it free

 

Dragon Powers by R.Q.

 

Design, Consider, and Plot

A Skipping Stone

With three stops

Bouncing on the Blue Lake

That is my mind

 

In the depths of the earth

Rests the spirit of a dragon

 

In the depths of my soul

Rests the spirit of a dragon

 

The earth with its infinite wisdom

Never forces results

 

Those who have heard

The crashing shock

Of lightning

A moment of intensity

A persistent intensity

An eternity of intensity

 

Turmoil, Angst, and amongst

This rubble, optimism, positivity

Redemption, Transcedece, Maturation

 

The universe is unlimited potential

Gathering its momentum

The true dragon

Embodies Duration

Concentrated Gathering

Pooling and Welling

Thus it is said

That lightning

Originates in the

Earth

And the dragon

Soars to heaven

At the perfect moment

Illuminating the sky

And sending rippling

Vibrations through the

Air

 

Who can penetrate

Past what is External

Who can value

What is

Immaterial

Who is able

Walk on earth

As dragons

Soar the sky

 

The people

In the growing

Bureaucracy

Like a falling

Glass bound

To shatter

Like a crooked

Building

On the verge of collapse

Like cold ash

About to crumble

 

Purpose and Meaning

Contemplating about

States of Being

Is it my will

Or is it my idea?

Self perpetuation

The necessity

Of self expression

What can be

Understood?

 

After a time of decay

Comes a turning point

All the turmoil...

All the tranquility...

That which expands

Must first contract

Electricity requires

A powerful dipole

That which is separated

Must eventually recombine

 

We begin with dust

And some pieces

Cluster together

Gain momentum

And energy

Through revolution

And eventually

There is life

I am life

I am dust

I am all the space in between

I am all the time in between

The story is called my idea

The Dragon is called my will

The idea is vast

And the Dragon

Is Eternal

 

Venetian Mask by R.Q.

 

Stare deep into my

Venetian Mask

A visage cast

Out of metal

A soul cast

Out of mental

Anxieties

Born into

Trying times

All the times

Are trying times

Since that first

Eye opened

Since those

First limbs

Grazed

The surface

 

When I hit

My early twenties

Rosie vignettes,

Nonchalance

Gave way to

A heavy dose

Of Melancholy

A Solitary Snake

Motionless

For weeks

The Ruling class

Casting nets

To fish me up

Restrict me to

An inauthentic life

Of service time

Drama, domestic ties

Filial bonds

Like the nails

Pinning him

To the crucifix

Oedipus and Electra

Know that your

Children are not

Yours to own

Know that your

Body is a form

Given from heaven

Know that your

Life is the expression

Of universal creativity

 

The structure

Of your brain

Contains the

Entire history

Of creation

The subconscious

Mind

Is larger than

The Cosmos

 

Your directed thoughts

Panting, from exhaustion

This world given by senses

Is flimsy compared to

The Fantasies I've invented

Sincerity can't be forced

My Venetian mask

Is here to remind you of that

Our education system feels so

Lifeless, children are

Continually taking thier lifes

And the love is lacking

Everything has its proper

Timing and youth's

Neglected,

Teeth and claws

Snarling, Lust

Obesity

Excessive Indulgence

You want to educate

A workforce?

What about all

Wilted flowers you

Don't care for?

Grades and lack

Of social status

Automation

Empty roles

Empty souls

The despair in me

Reflected by the

Wealth disparity

Had me feeling

Worthless

How many men

How many women

How many children

Wake each morning

Feeling

Hopelessly Worthless

 

Thankfully, I've got

A lot to grateful for

Blessed by way of

The Pen and Sword

The Melancholy

Hit hard in my

Early Twenties

Had to mine my

Subconscious

For a revelation

Third eye Chakra

Activation

Steady breath

Consistency

Continuously

Conscientious

Practice

Absolution

Deliverance
